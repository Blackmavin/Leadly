WHO ARE WE?

Leadly provides business development services to B2B companies of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.

With our strategic solutions; acquire more customers, save costs and time spent on sales research, prospecting, generating and qualifying leads.

1.

Prospecting

We generate a customized list of a minimum of 500
prospect records that focuses on your requirements.
With our strategic tools, we provide the following within
the target companies:

  • Company names.
  • Company Address.
  • Contact details

2.

Prospecting Plus

With this service, Leadly assists your in-house
sales team in generating a minimum of 100
quality prospects and provides key details.
This includes:

  • Company name.
  • Company address.
  • Funding status of prospect.
  • Growth stage of prospect(Micro-Business/SME/Enterprise).
  • Sector classification.
  • Contact details of decision makers: emails and phone numbers*
  • Links to recent news.
  • Other information based on context.

3.

Book a call

This service ensures that a prospect schedule a
meeting with you. Take the burden of constantly
trying to book meetings with clients off your sales
team. Here’s what this covers:

  • Prospecting.
  • Prospecting plus.
  • Cold calling.
  • Cold emails and personalization.
  • CRM follow-up and tracking.
  • 10 booked meetings with prospects per month.

4.


Qualified leads

This involves getting a prospective client to a
ready-to-buy position. Our team will work as an
integral part of the sales process for your business.
Here’s what this entails:

  • Prospecting.
  • Prospecting Plus.
  • Book a call.
  • Usecase discovery and compatibility.
  • Prospect’s budget.
  • Readiness meter and timeliness.
  • Quality rating system.
  • At least two meetings with each lead.
  • 25 qualified leads per quarter.
